Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Data Storage and User Identity

  • Personal Data: We collect your name and email address only via Sign in with Apple for personalized greetings and internal account management.
  • Data Storage (Privacy First): No user data, including images, pose coordinates, or analysis results, is stored on our remote servers. All personal, scan, and progress data is secured within your personal iCloud/CloudKit storage.
  • We do not collect, store, or process any facial recognition data or face biometric data. The app uses body pose detection technology to analyze posture by detecting body joint positions only.

2. How Scanning Data is Processed

  • Image Storage: The images you capture for analysis are saved only to your personal and secure iCloud/CloudKit storage. We do not have access to them.
  • AI Processing: To generate the advanced posture analysis (scores, insights), we send a base64 encoded version of your scan image to the OpenAI API for processing.
  • API Data Privacy: According to OpenAI's API data usage policy, the image data sent for analysis is not stored on their servers or used for training their AI models. It is processed immediately to generate your analysis and then discarded.
  • Analytics Tracking: We use Firebase Analytics (a third-party tool) to track high-level product usage, specifically anonymized answers from the initial onboarding questionnaire. This data is used solely for product improvement (e.g., refining exercise recommendations). This data is not linked to your user ID, Apple ID, or any other personally identifiable information.
