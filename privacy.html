Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

1. Data Storage and User Identity

  • Personal Data: We collect your name and email address only via Sign in with Apple for personalized greetings and internal account management.
  • Data Storage (Privacy First): No user data, including images, pose coordinates, or analysis results, is stored on our remote servers. All personal, scan, and progress data is secured within your personal iCloud/CloudKit storage.

2. How Scanning Data is Processed

  • Image Handling: The live video feed is processed on your device using Apple's Vision framework to detect key anatomical joint coordinates. The raw image is saved only to your local/iCloud storage.
  • AI Processing: To generate the advanced posture analysis (scores, insights), we send a small JSON object containing only numerical coordinate data (relative X/Y positions of joints) to the OpenAI API. We never send raw images or videos to OpenAI or any third-party server.
  • Coordinate Data: This coordinate data is instantly analyzed by the AI and is not logged or stored by the external API provider for our records.
